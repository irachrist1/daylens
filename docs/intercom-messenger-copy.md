# Daylens Messenger copy (Intercom dashboard config, not articles)

These are set in the Intercom dashboard, not in code. Paste them where noted.

## Messenger greeting

Intercom → **Messenger → Content / Home** (the greeting shown at the top of the
Messenger):

> **Hey 👋 I'm Daylens AI.**
> Ask me anything about setting up, tracking your time, or how your data stays private
> — I answer right away, and the team's here too.

Shorter header subtitle (the line under the agent name):

> Ask me anything — the team can help too.

## Suggested questions

Intercom → **AI / Fin → Suggested questions** (or Messenger → Home → suggested/quick
questions). These seed the chips users can tap instead of typing — pick 4–6:

- What can Daylens do?
- How do I turn on AI features?
- Is my activity data private?
- How do I grant permission on my Mac?
- My day looks wrong — how do I fix it?
- How do I pause tracking or exclude an app?

## When Fin hands off to the team

Intercom → **AI / Fin → behaviour when Fin can't answer** (the message shown before
routing to a human):

> I'll pass this to the Daylens team — you'll get a reply right here and by email.

## Notes

- The onboarding "Questions?" button and the Settings → Help & support "Chat with us"
  button both open this same Messenger, so this copy covers every entry point.
- Keep the greeting first-person as "Daylens AI" only after you've renamed the agent from
  "Fin" (Intercom → AI / Fin → name). Until then it'll still say Fin.
